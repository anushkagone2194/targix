Resources & Tips

Resume

Keep it concise, emphasize impact (numbers), and list technologies used. Put projects and contributions first.

Behavioral Interview

Use STAR (Situation, Task, Action, Result) for every story. Prepare 4–6 stories: leadership, teamwork, failure, accomplishment.

Coding Interview

Practice patterns: two-pointers, sliding window, DFS/BFS, dynamic programming, greedy. Explain approach first, then code.
